Project Summary

CONTINENTAL WICK is the official token and collector system of the CONTINENTAL protocol — a universe of protocol enforcers, vault agents, and signal operators running on $XRP rails.

$WCK sits at the center of a structured ecosystem:

  • The token layer for access, alignment, and protocol actions.
  • A Collector Card NFT series that turns protocol roles into character-driven digital collectibles.

The objective is clear: build a living collector series on XRPL — evolving characters, escalating threats, and expanding sets that feel like a real card universe, not a random collection of drops.

Role of $WCK

$WCK operates as the native asset of the CONTINENTAL protocol in three primary ways:

Access and Alignment

  • Enables priority access to future CONTINENTAL drops and ecosystem expansions.
  • Acts as a community alignment layer for ecosystem direction at the discretion of the team.

Collector and Protocol Utility

  • Used as a key reference asset around future card drops, protocol events, and gated experiences.
  • Eligible to be integrated into collector mechanics such as raffles, draws, or snapshot-based recognition where legally permissible.

Ecosystem Sync

  • Ties the narrative together across NFT series, physical integrations, and protocol-grade tools built on XRPL.

CONTINENTAL WICK Collector Cards (NFTs)

$WCK powers a series-based Collector Card universe on XRPL. Each card is an NFT representing a protocol role inside the CONTINENTAL world.

Core Structure

  • Network: XRPL
  • Standard: XRPL NFT (NFToken)

  • Series / Sets: built in waves and series, designed as a true collector line with base characters, variants, threat types, and protocol ranks.

Card 001 — CONTINENTAL WICK — anchors the story as the first ultra-rare protocol card. Subsequent cards expand the world: vault security, armory specialists, surveillance nodes, internal operators, and external threats.

Technology Stack

CONTINENTAL WICK is built on the XRP Ledger (XRPL) to leverage:

  • Speed — near-instant settlement and confirmations.
  • Cost Control — low, predictable transaction fees.
  • Deterministic Execution — no gas auctions or mempool warfare.
  • Native DEX and NFT Support — built-in order books, AMMs, and NFT primitives.

This stack keeps execution clean and efficient for both the $WCK token and the Collector Card series.
